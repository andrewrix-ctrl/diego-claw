Bostik Blu Tack Original

Premium product-page refresh, including Drew's photo and a concise product history.

Product profile

Stick, position, hold

Bostik Blu Tack Original is a reusable pressure-sensitive adhesive putty for temporary placement and light hold tasks, especially on posters, decorations, and desk accessories.

Brand

Bostik

Variant

Blu Tack Original

Pack shown

75g

Made in

Australia (as marked on pack)

Key uses

  • Temporary poster and decoration mounting.
  • Holding down lightweight desk accessories.
  • Cleaning fluff and dust from keyboards or small crevices.
  • General repositionable sticking for home, school, or office.

History timeline

  1. 1969 (UK): Developed during sealant research using a chalk, rubber, and oil blend.
  2. 1970: Commercial rollout as a reusable adhesive putty product.
  3. Late 1970s: Arrived in Australia, later produced at Bostik's Thomastown (Victoria) site.
  4. 2003: Blu Tack Colour introduced for craft and play.
  5. Today: A long-running household adhesive product across everyday "quick fix" scenarios.
