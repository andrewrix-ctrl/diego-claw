Bostik Blu Tack Original (75g)

A quick product profile and history page created from Drew's photo of the retail pack.

Product Snapshot

Bostik Blu Tack is a reusable pressure-sensitive adhesive putty designed for temporary sticking, positioning, and holding without permanent residue when used correctly.

Brand

Bostik

Product

Blu Tack Original

Pack size (photo)

75g

Common uses

Posters, decorations, cable/accessory hold-down, keyboard fluff pick-up

What this pack highlights

  • Reusable adhesive for day-to-day home, school, and office tasks.
  • Promoted use cases on-pack include holding posters and decorations, cleaning fluff/dust, and light desk organisation.
  • "Stick, Position, Hold" messaging signals easy repositioning before final placement.

Short history

  1. 1969 (UK): Developed during sealant research as an accidental by-product (chalk powder, rubber, and oil base).
  2. 1970: Commercial launch period for Blu Tack as a reusable household adhesive.
  3. Late 1970s: Expansion to Australia, with local manufacturing later established at Bostik's Thomastown, Victoria plant.
  4. 2003: Blu Tack Colour introduced as an additional variant for craft and play use.
  5. Today: Still widely used for temporary mounting and practical "tiny fix" tasks at home and work.

Sources: Bostik Australia (Blu Tack page), Bostik Australia media note on Blu Tack anniversary.
