FAQ

Do I need a referral?
No referral is needed for a private appointment. If you have a GP chronic disease management plan, bring the paperwork so eligible visits can be processed.
Can I claim private health insurance rebates?
Many extras policies include podiatry. Rebate amount depends on your cover, so check with your health fund before your appointment.
What should I bring to my first appointment?
Bring your usual shoes (work and sport), relevant scans or reports, and a medication list or medical summary if available.
What happens during a first visit?
We discuss your symptoms, check foot and lower-limb function, watch your standing/walking pattern, and explain your treatment plan in plain language.
Do you see children?
Yes. Kids' appointments are relaxed and age-appropriate, with practical advice for school, sport, and everyday footwear.
Are home visits available?
Home visits may be available in Glebe and nearby suburbs by arrangement. Call the clinic to discuss suitability and availability.
