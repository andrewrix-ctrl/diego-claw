Who you'll see

Bethan Kinning, Principal Podiatrist

Beth provides practical, plain-language care for people of all ages, with focus areas including biomechanics, skin and nail care, and long-term foot health planning.

Every appointment includes a clear assessment, treatment options that fit your lifestyle, and a plan you can actually follow.

What we help with

Heel and arch pain

Plantar fasciitis, overload pain, and recurring soreness from work or sport.

Skin and nails

Ingrown nails, corns, callus, cracked heels, and ongoing foot care.

Kids' feet

Walking patterns, pain with sport, and footwear advice for growing feet.

Diabetes checks

Regular assessments to reduce risk and spot early warning signs.
