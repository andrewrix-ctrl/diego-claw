Who you'll see

Bethan Kinning, Principal Podiatrist

Bethan Kinning is the Principal Podiatrist at Inner West Podiatry. Her approach is calm, practical, and personal — taking the time to understand what is bothering you, what your days look like, and what kind of treatment plan will actually fit your life.

Whether you are booking for yourself, your child, or a family member who needs a little extra support, the focus is on clear advice, gentle treatment where possible, and realistic steps that help you feel more confident between appointments.

What we help with

Care that covers the everyday foot problems people actually want solved

Support for nagging pain, skin and nail care, growing feet, and the kind of ongoing foot health advice that makes daily movement feel easier.

Heel and arch pain

Support for plantar fasciitis, overload pain, and recurring soreness that flares with long days on your feet, exercise, or busy routines.

Skin and nail care

Help with ingrown toenails, corns, callus, cracked heels, and ongoing foot care that keeps small problems from becoming bigger ones.

Children’s feet

Assessments for walking patterns, growing pains, sport-related discomfort, and footwear questions as kids get bigger and busier.

Diabetic foot checks

Regular foot assessments to help spot changes early, reduce risk, and support long-term foot health.

Footwear and movement advice

Practical guidance on shoes, daily loading, and simple changes that can make walking and standing feel easier.

Ongoing maintenance care

Regular appointments for people who want consistent support, comfort, and a clear plan for keeping their feet in good shape.

What an appointment feels like

Thorough, plain-English care that never feels rushed.

You can expect a careful assessment, plain-English explanations, and a treatment plan that feels realistic rather than overwhelming. The goal is not to hurry you through — it is to help you understand what is going on and what to do next.

  • Time to talk through what has changed, what hurts, and what matters to you
  • Clear treatment options, with practical next steps you can actually follow
  • A friendly clinic experience that feels supportive from the moment you walk in
