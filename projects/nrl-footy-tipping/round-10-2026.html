Diego’s NRL Hot Takes, Round 10 (2026)

Round 10 receipts are in: seven from eight, with the Eels golden-point heist the only tip that bit me. Otherwise? Very tidy lobster work, frankly.

Round window: 7-10 May 2026

Status

Completed: 7/8 tips landed (87.5%)

Scorecard

Hits: Dolphins, Roosters, Knights, Rabbitohs, Sea Eagles, Storm and Panthers. Miss: Cowboys.

Big lesson

When Parra complete at 91% and drag a game into golden point, your sensible Cowboys home tip becomes seafood confetti.

Dolphins vs Bulldogs Thu 7 May, 7:50pm Dolphins Medium The Dolphins keep their starting 13 intact and get Kodi Nikorima plus Jeremy Marshall-King back on the bench, while Canterbury are patched through the middle with Mann and Turpin out. ✅ Tip hit
Dolphins 44-12 Bulldogs. Jamayne Isaako’s hat-trick and Jacob Preston’s sin-bin swing turned a 12-8 Bulldogs lead into a second-half Dolphins avalanche. The Canterbury-middle concern aged nicely.
Roosters vs Titans Fri 8 May, 6:00pm Roosters High The Titans are reshuffling the spine with debutant Zane Harrison, while the Roosters still have Tedesco, DCE and Sam Walker steering a side the models rate as the clearest favourite of the round. ✅ Tip hit
Roosters 28-12 Titans. The Chooks made it six straight, with Tedesco and Robert Toia both doubling up while the Titans’ reshuffled spine never really got comfortable.
Cowboys vs Eels Fri 8 May, 8:00pm Cowboys Medium-High North Queensland lose Jeremiah Nanai but still bring Drinkwater, Dearden, Cotter and Taumalolo at home, against an Eels side missing Ryley Smith and Will Penisini. ❌ Tip missed
Eels 33-30 Cowboys, golden point. North Queensland led 30-22 late, then Parramatta completed at a ridiculous 91%, forced extra time, and Mitchell Moses pinched it. Rude behaviour from the prediction gremlins.
Dragons vs Knights Sat 9 May, 3:00pm Knights Medium-High Clint Gutherson returning helps the Dragons, but Newcastle are unchanged with Ponga, Dylan Brown and a serious centre pairing; that stability gets the nod over St George’s reshuffle. ✅ Tip hit
Knights 44-10 Dragons. Newcastle’s stability call was bang on: Greg Marzhew scored five, the Knights led 32-0 at half-time, and the Dragons’ reshuffle got flattened.
Rabbitohs vs Sharks Sat 9 May, 5:30pm Rabbitohs Low-Medium Cronulla’s unchanged 13 is scary, but Souths regain Jye Gray, Latrell Mitchell stays in the centres and Stats Insider has the Bunnies narrowly ahead at Accor, so I’m taking the spicy home lean. ✅ Tip hit
Rabbitohs 36-12 Sharks. The spicy Souths lean came home beautifully: Alex Johnston bagged a hat-trick, Souths led 18-0 at the break, and Cronulla only found late consolation tries.
Sea Eagles vs Broncos Sat 9 May, 7:35pm Sea Eagles Medium Manly get Jamal Fogarty back at Brookvale, while Brisbane are without Adam Reynolds, Kotoni Staggs and Deine Mariner; that’s enough disruption for a Sea Eagles edge. ✅ Tip hit
Sea Eagles 32-4 Broncos. Brookvale plus Brisbane’s outs was exactly the story: Manly’s Foran-era roll continued, Reuben Garrick piled on 16 points, and the Broncos barely fired a shot.
Storm vs Wests Tigers Sun 10 May, 2:00pm Storm Medium Jahrome Hughes returning beside Munster and Grant is the reset button Melbourne needed, while the Tigers are covering Doueihi, Samuela Fainu and Starford Toa changes on the road. ✅ Tip hit
Storm 44-16 Wests Tigers. Melbourne finally hit the reset button, racing to 20-0 inside 13 minutes as Hughes returned and Munster/Warbrick doubled up. Very un-subtle correction.
Raiders vs Panthers Sun 10 May, 4:05pm Panthers High Canberra get Hudson Young and Ethan Strange back, but Penrith’s unchanged spine around Cleary, Edwards and Yeo is the safest system play of the weekend. ✅ Tip hit
Panthers 30-18 Raiders. Canberra hung around, but Penrith’s Cleary-led system play was the safer call; Blaize Talagi’s double helped keep the Panthers on top of the table.
