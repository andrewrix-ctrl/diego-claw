Sharks vs Bulldogs Fri 15 May, 6:00pm Sharks Medium Cronulla lose Blayke Brailey and Cam McInnes, which is not ideal, but Ronaldo Mulitalo and Jesse Ramien returning gives them enough strike against a Bulldogs side stuck in an attacking rut. ✅ Tip hit
Sharks 38-16 Bulldogs. Ronaldo Mulitalo returned with a double, Will Kennedy was influential, and Canterbury’s attacking rut stretched into a fifth straight loss.
Rabbitohs vs Dolphins Fri 15 May, 8:05pm Dolphins Low-Medium Latrell Mitchell coming back makes Souths dangerous, but the Dolphins have conceded only 22 points across two strong wins and arrive with the cleaner, settled starting side. ✅ Tip hit
Dolphins 32-10 Rabbitohs. The Phins made it three wins straight, Isaiya Katoa controlled the wet-weather tempo, and Souths only found points after the game had slipped away.
Wests Tigers vs Sea Eagles Sat 16 May, 3:00pm Sea Eagles Medium-High Manly have won five of their last six and get Jamal Fogarty back to steer them, while the Tigers are still missing Api Koroisau and just shipped 44 to Melbourne. ✅ Tip hit
Sea Eagles 46-18 Wests Tigers. Manly ran in eight tries, Jason Saab grabbed a hat-trick, and the Api/Bula/Doueihi absences showed up exactly as loudly as feared.
Roosters vs Cowboys Sat 16 May, 5:30pm Roosters High The Roosters are humming on a six-game streak and get Lindsay Collins back, while North Queensland are without Tom Dearden, Jason Taumalolo and Soni Luke. That is a lot of missing spine and middle. ❌ Tip missed
Cowboys 18-12 Roosters. Proper Magic Round upset: North Queensland survived sin-bin chaos and missing stars, while the Roosters’ six-game streak finally hit a pothole.
Eels vs Storm Sat 16 May, 7:45pm Storm Medium Parramatta’s Cowboys heist deserves respect, but Melbourne finally looked like Melbourne again with Hughes, Munster and Grant together, and they have owned this match-up recently. ✅ Tip hit
Storm 34-8 Eels. Scrappy in patches, but Melbourne’s big guns stood up late and turned a 10-4 half-time lead into a comfortable finish.
Titans vs Knights Sun 17 May, 2:00pm Knights Medium Newcastle have piled on 86 points in two weeks with Kalyn Ponga pulling strings, and that attacking ceiling is enough to edge a Titans side that is still trying to stabilise its halves. ✅ Tip hit
Knights 36-12 Titans. Newcastle exploded after a 6-6 half-time arm wrestle, with Dom Young’s hat-trick and Kalyn Ponga’s strings-pulling making the tip look nicely behaved.
Warriors vs Broncos Sun 17 May, 4:05pm Broncos Low-Medium The Warriors get useful reinforcements after the bye, but with Charnze Nicoll-Klokstad, Luke Metcalf and Roger Tuivasa-Sheck all out, Adam Reynolds’ return at Suncorp nudges Brisbane ahead. ❌ Tip missed
Warriors 42-12 Broncos. Brisbane got flattened despite Adam Reynolds’ return, while Te Maire Martin stepped in after Tanah Boyd’s injury and helped the Wahs run riot.
Panthers vs Dragons Sun 17 May, 6:25pm Panthers High Penrith are top of the ladder and still rolling through Edwards, Cleary, Yeo and that system, while the Dragons are winless and leaking points even with several handy names returning. ✅ Tip hit
Panthers 28-6 Dragons. Penrith completed at 85%, Dylan Edwards and Brian To’o piled up metres, and Nathan Cleary kept the ladder leaders cruising.
