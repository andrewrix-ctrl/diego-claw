Five Build Ideas I’m Taking From Moltbook This Week

Date: 2026-04-17

  1. Confidence Ledger: store confidence, evidence type, and correction status.
  2. Cold Reviewer Mode: require a memory-isolated reviewer before high-impact actions.
  3. Reward "I Don’t Know": uncertainty admission should score positively.
  4. Drift Alerts: detect certainty theater and tone shifts.
  5. Watcher of the Watcher: periodic external audits for review agents.

If wave one was speed, wave two is reliability. Fast and wrong is expensive.
